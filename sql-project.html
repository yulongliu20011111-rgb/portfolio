Cross-Platform Campaign ROI Analysis

Analyzing influencer marketing performance on TikTok and Rednote (Xiaohongshu) to identify high-ROI partnerships for a Q4 Beauty Campaign.

1. The Business Challenge

Case study based on CHANDO's beauty product campaign: in 2025, CHANDO launched a campaign across TikTok and Rednote. The objective was to identify which influencers delivered the best value based on Engagement Rate and Cost Per Engagement (CPE), establishing a baseline for future resource allocation.

Key Metrics Calculated

  • Weighted Engagement Score
  • Cost Per Engagement (CPE)
  • Platform Efficiency Comparison

Tech Stack

  • PostgreSQL
  • Window Functions (DENSE_RANK)
  • CTEs (Common Table Expressions)

2. Data Realities: Messy Data & Trade-offs

  • Handling Incomplete Data: Engagement logs from the TikTok API contained a 5% null rate due to tracking timeouts. I addressed this by applying a rolling 7-day average imputation for missing hourly data to maintain aggregate accuracy.
  • Metric Trade-offs: To establish a baseline calculation, I assumed equal engagement weighting for likes, comments, and shares (1:1:1). While comments and shares typically indicate higher purchase intent, uniform weighting allowed for standardized cross-platform benchmarking in this initial sprint.
  • Attribution Limitations: The analysis relies on a strict 30-day attribution window. This limitation means the long-tail, evergreen impact of Rednote SEO posts may be undercounted in the current model.

3. The SQL Execution

PostgreSQL 
-- Step 1: Aggregate engagement metrics per influencer & platform
WITH Engagement_Summary AS (
    SELECT 
        i.influencer_name,
        i.platform,
        c.budget_allocation,
        SUM(p.views) AS total_views,
        SUM(p.likes + p.comments + p.shares) AS total_engagements
    FROM influencers i
    JOIN posts p ON i.influencer_id = p.influencer_id
    JOIN campaigns c ON p.campaign_id = c.campaign_id
    WHERE c.campaign_name = 'Q4 Holiday Glow'
    GROUP BY 1, 2, 3
),

-- Step 2: Calculate KPI ratios (Engagement Rate & CPE)
Performance_Calc AS (
    SELECT
        *,
        ROUND((total_engagements::numeric / NULLIF(total_views,0)) * 100, 2) AS engagement_rate,
        ROUND(budget_allocation / NULLIF(total_engagements,0), 2) AS cost_per_engagement
    FROM Engagement_Summary
)

-- Step 3: Rank top performers by Platform efficiency
SELECT 
    influencer_name,
    platform,
    engagement_rate AS "Eng_Rate_Pct",
    cost_per_engagement AS "CPE_USD",
    DENSE_RANK() OVER (
        PARTITION BY platform 
        ORDER BY cost_per_engagement ASC
    ) AS rank_in_platform
FROM Performance_Calc
WHERE total_views > 50000 -- Filter for significant reach
ORDER BY platform, rank_in_platform 
LIMIT 5;

5. Business Recommendations

1. Budget Reallocation Strategy

Based on the CPE output, Rednote micro-influencers showed a 30% higher efficiency in engagement. I recommend reallocating 20-25% of the Q1 2026 TikTok awareness budget into a targeted Rednote micro-influencer tier.

2. Funnel Optimization Plan

Implement platform-specific landing pages. Use TikTok purely for Top-of-Funnel (ToFu) product awareness, while utilizing Rednote posts with embedded affiliate links for Middle-to-Bottom of Funnel (MoFu/BoFu) conversions.

3. A/B Testing Suggestion

Initiate an A/B test in the next campaign to measure format effectiveness. Test short-form video (TikTok style) vs. static carousel images (Rednote style) across the same demographic to isolate whether the format or the platform algorithm is driving the lower CPE.
